The Calculation Rules

Our formula perfectly aligns words to the grid without leaving loose margins:

  • Horizontal: $2M + 5L + (L - 1)$ where $M$ is the 2-grid margin and $L$ is letter length.
  • Vertical: $2M + 5N + 2(N - 1)$ where $N$ is total stacked rows of text.
  • The Unit Rule: Each cell represents exactly one inch of space.
